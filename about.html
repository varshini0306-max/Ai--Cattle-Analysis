About CattleAI

We are a team of agricultural technologists and AI researchers dedicated to transforming cattle health monitoring through intelligent automation.

Our Mission

Healthier Herds Through Smarter Technology

CattleAI was founded with a singular mission: to give every farmer — regardless of the size of their operation — access to the same level of precision health monitoring once reserved for large industrial farms with expensive specialists.

Using state-of-the-art convolutional neural networks (CNN) and long short-term memory models (LSTM), our platform analyses cattle gait patterns from standard video or image uploads, detecting early signs of lameness and disease before they become costly, painful problems.

Our technology is built on thousands of validated cattle movement samples, continuously improving with every new analysis — meaning the more farmers use it, the smarter and more accurate it becomes.
