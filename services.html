Our Services

End-to-end cattle health monitoring solutions — from real-time gait analysis to disease detection, health reports, and farm-level insights.

What We Offer

Complete Health Monitoring Suite

Every tool a modern livestock farm needs in one unified platform.

🎯
Gait Analysis
Video & Image Processing

Upload video or still images of walking cattle. Our computer vision pipeline extracts body key points, calculates stride metrics, and identifies movement anomalies associated with pain or discomfort.

  • Stride length, step frequency and walking speed
  • Left-right symmetry analysis per limb
  • Weight distribution across all 4 legs
  • Joint movement angle extraction
  • Temporal consistency scoring (video only)
✅ Real-Time
🔬
Disease Detection
CNN Classification Engine

Our deep learning classifier identifies 7 common cattle foot and joint conditions, providing a probability score and recommended course of action for each detection.

  • Digital Dermatitis detection
  • Sole Ulcer and White Line Disease
  • Laminitis and joint infection
  • Hoof overgrowth identification
  • Confidence score per diagnosis
🔬 AI Powered
📊
Lameness Scoring
0–5 Severity Scale

Automated lameness scoring aligned with the internationally recognised Sprecher scale. Each analysis returns a severity level with a detailed explanation and recommended intervention.

  • 0–5 lameness severity classification
  • Automated vet referral triggers at score ≥ 3
  • Trend tracking per individual animal
  • Batch scoring for entire herd videos
  • Export scores to farm management software
📈 Score Tracking
📋
Health Reports
Detailed PDF & Dashboard

Every analysis generates a shareable health report with all gait metrics, disease probabilities, recommended treatments, and follow-up scheduling. Share directly with your veterinarian.

  • Full gait feature breakdown
  • Disease classification with confidence
  • Treatment recommendations
  • Historical comparison charts
  • One-click PDF export and sharing
