Help Centre

Common Questions

What is CattleAI and how does it work?
▼
CattleAI is an AI-powered cattle health monitoring platform. You upload a photo or video of your cattle, and our deep learning models analyse their gait to detect signs of lameness, disease, or discomfort. Results are delivered in seconds with a detailed health report.
Do I need any special equipment to use CattleAI?
▼
No special equipment required. A standard smartphone camera or farm CCTV footage works perfectly. We recommend filming from the side while the animal walks in a straight line, but our system handles various angles and lighting conditions effectively.
How accurate is the disease detection?
▼
Overall accuracy across all detectable conditions is 95%. Individual disease accuracy varies from 89% (joint infection) to 98% (hoof overgrowth). Our models are validated against veterinary-confirmed diagnoses and continuously improved with new data.
Is CattleAI a replacement for a veterinarian?
▼
No. CattleAI is a screening and monitoring tool, not a veterinary service. It helps you identify animals that need attention much earlier than manual observation would allow. All high-severity detections should be confirmed and treated by a qualified veterinarian.
Can I use CattleAI for beef cattle, not just dairy?
▼
Yes. CattleAI works for all cattle breeds — dairy, beef, and dual-purpose. Our models were trained on diverse cattle populations and automatically adjust reference ranges based on visual characteristics.
What file formats and sizes are supported?
▼
Images: JPG, JPEG, PNG (up to 100MB). Videos: MP4, AVI, MOV (up to 100MB). For video, we recommend at least 5–10 seconds showing the animal walking. Higher resolution footage generally produces more accurate results.
What AI models power the analysis?
▼
We use a ResNet50-based CNN for feature extraction and disease classification, and an LSTM network for temporal gait analysis in videos. The pipeline runs on TensorFlow 2.x and processes frames through OpenCV before feeding into the neural networks.
How long does an analysis take?
▼
Image analysis typically completes in 3–5 seconds. Video analysis takes 8–15 seconds depending on video length and resolution. We guarantee results within 60 seconds.
Is there a mobile app?
▼
A dedicated iOS and Android app is currently in development and expected in Q3 2026. Until then, our web platform is fully responsive and works smoothly on mobile browsers — you can upload directly from your phone camera.
What does the lameness score (0–5) mean?
▼
0 = No lameness. 1 = Mild (slight irregularity). 2 = Moderate (visible limp, arched back). 3 = Significant (pronounced limp). 4 = Severe (major gait disturbance). 5 = Critical (non-weight-bearing, requires immediate veterinary care).
What gait features does CattleAI measure?
▼
We measure: stride length (metres), step frequency (steps/minute), walking speed (m/s), gait symmetry ratio, weight distribution across all 4 limbs, joint movement angles, and temporal consistency (video only). All values are compared against breed-specific reference ranges.
Can I track an individual animal over time?
▼
Yes. Our History page stores all past analyses linked to your account. Future updates will include individual animal tagging (ear tag / RFID integration) so you can view full longitudinal health records per animal.
Is there a free trial?
▼
Yes. Every new account receives 10 free analyses — no credit card required. This allows you to test the platform with your own cattle before choosing a paid plan.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
▼
Yes, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime from your account settings — your access continues until the end of the billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
▼
We accept UPI, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and bank transfers for enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely through Razorpay. GST invoices are issued automatically for Indian businesses.
